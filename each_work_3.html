Catergories: Animation, 3D Work, Individual Work

Duration: 1 month

Summary: Taking inspiration from “Chi Pheo” and “Son Tinh Thuy Tinh”, Vietnamese fictional characters, I aim to create a character who experiences disbelief and rejection from society. Additionally, this character also maintain some traits of an hero. To convey this complexity, the character design will incorporate various items that reflect both dark personality traits and deeper inner struggles.

"Dứa Sẹo"

“Dứa Sẹo”, 16-year-old boy living in North Western of Vietnam, with witch blood running through his veins. He possesses a magical glove and ‘sacoche’ that allows him to control the movement of water, which he uses to save people from drowning and extinguish forest fires...
