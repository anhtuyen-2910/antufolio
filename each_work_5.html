Catergories: UX-UI Work, Teamwork

Responsibility: Character Design, Button Design

Duration: 1 month

Summary: “Wacky Chicky” is an interactive digital infographic that walks users through the process of building a farm in seven steps. Through this project, we aim to provide viewers with an educational and entertaining experience with a variety of interactions.

Wacky Chicky

In terms of illustration, we employ flat shading style and isometric perspective art with a farm background to make two-dimensional figures look more like three-dimensional ones. In addition, our layout simulates a game with a general map where players’ activities take place.
